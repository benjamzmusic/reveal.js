Amazon Transcribe
Speech to text capability for applications
Easy to read transcriptions, vocabulary filtering
Also recognizes multiple speakers
Amazon Translate
Machine translation service for affordable and high quality language translation
Highly accurate and continously learning
Easy and fast to localize your App/Website
Amazon Polly
Service that turns text into lifelike speech
Uses Advanced Deep Learning to produce lifelike speech
Low Cost, "Pay as you go"
